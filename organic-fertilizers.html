Organic Fertilizers

Sustainable soil nutrition solutions for the next-generation farmer.

The Mission

Restoring Soil Health with Nature's Power

At Gyanti Multiservices, we believe that healthy crops start with healthy soil. Our organic fertilizer production unit converts livestock waste and organic farm residues into high-nutrient soil conditioners.

We use advanced composting and fermentation techniques to ensure that our fertilizers are rich in microbial life and essential plant nutrients.

  • 100% Chemical-Free Formulation
  • Enhanced Microbial Activity
  • Improves Water Retention
  • Waste-to-Wealth Model
Our Process

How We Create Liquid Gold

Collection

Raw organic waste is collected from our integrated dairy and poultry units.

Aeration

The waste is aerated and mixed to initiate natural aerobic decomposition.

Maturation

Substances mature over 60-90 days to develop a rich, earthy compost structure.

Packaging

Final products are screened, tested for quality, and packed for distribution.
