Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Here's how we protect your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when you contact us through our website, subscribe to our newsletter, or use our farming consultation services. This may include your name, email address, phone number, and location.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to provide, maintain, and improve our services, communicate with you about agricultural updates, and ensure the security of our platform.

3. Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information when you enter, submit, or access your data. We do not sell or trade your personal information to third parties.

4. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience and analyze site traffic. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings.
