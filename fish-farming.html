Fish Farming (Machli)

Advanced aquaculture and sustainable fisheries management.

Modern Aquaculture

At Gyanti Multiservices, we leverage high-density fish farming technologies like Bio-floc and RAS to produce healthy, organic fish with minimal environmental impact.

Our Technical Capabilities

  • RAS Technology: Recirculating Aquaculture Systems for 90% water reuse and high hygiene.
  • Bio-floc Systems: Natural waste recycling into high-protein fish feed within the tanks.
  • Water Quality Monitoring: Real-time tracking of pH, oxygen, and ammonia levels.
  • Aeration Solutions: High-efficiency aeration systems to ensure optimal oxygenation.

Water Efficiency

Our systems use 80% less water compared to traditional pond farming methods.

Purity Control

Zero antibiotic usage and hormone-free growth for the healthiest fish.
