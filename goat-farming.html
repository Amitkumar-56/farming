Goat Farming (Bakra)

Modern breeding and sustainable management for high-quality livestock.

Modern Goat Husbandry

At Gyanti Multiservices, we specialize in high-yield goat farming systems that prioritize animal health and sustainable production for maximum profitability.

Strategic Farming Plan

  • Breed Selection: Using superior breeds like Boer, Beetal, and Sirohi for high meat and milk yield.
  • Nutrition Management: Custom-formulated feed plans for optimal growth and immunity.
  • Climate Control: Modern housing designed to maintain ideal temperatures and ventilation.
  • Veterinary Care: Regular health checkups and vaccination schedules.

Market Value

Goat farming is one of the fastest-growing livestock sectors with consistent market demand.

Sustainability

Low environmental footprint with high utility—from milk and meat to high-quality manure.
